---

## 📂 Proposals Index

### 🌾 [[Direction 1 - Agri-Credit Scoring|Direction 1: Alternative-Data Agri-Credit Scoring]]
* **Unique Angle**: Translating offline paper records and agricultural/climate coordinates into creditworthiness.
* **Core Tech**: Mobile OCR + Satellite NDVI APIs + Collaborative Peer Trust Scoring.
* **Primary Risk**: Field agent compliance and hand-written script text recognition accuracy.

### 🏪 [[Direction 2 - Voice-Led Micro-Ledger|Direction 2: Voice-Led Micro-Ledger for Retailers]]
* **Unique Angle**: Bookkeeping through zero-UI conversational speech.
* **Core Tech**: WhatsApp/Viber Bot + Multilingual Speech-to-Text (ASR) + Entity-Extracting LLM.
* **Primary Risk**: Public market noise interference and low-resource dialect parsing.

### 🌦️ [[Direction 3 - Parametric Micro-Insurance|Direction 3: Parametric Micro-Insurance Registry]]
* **Unique Angle**: Zero-claims disaster payout triggered automatically by weather indices.
* **Core Tech**: Parametric Weather Oracles + Privacy-Preserving Shared Registries + Mobile Money APIs.
* **Primary Risk**: Basis risk (local damage occurring without crossing regional sensor triggers).

### 🧠 [[Direction 4 - Subjective Stress Tracker|Direction 4: Subjective Well-being & Stress Tracker]]
* **Unique Angle**: Evaluating and mitigating the psychological and emotional burden of debt.
* **Core Tech**: In-person group check-in surveys + NLP Speech/Text Sentiment Analysis + Decision Alert Engine.
* **Primary Risk**: Data privacy protection and ethical limits of scoring subjective emotional distress.
