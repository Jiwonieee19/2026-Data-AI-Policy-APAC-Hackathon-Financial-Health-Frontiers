# 💡 Hackathon Ideas: Brainstorming & Evaluation

This file lists the current proposed concepts for the Financial Health Hackathon, mapping their core technologies, target demographics, and strategic alignment.

---

## 📌 Proposed Concepts

We have brainstormed **4 core concepts** designed to improve the financial health of underserved communities in the Asia-Pacific region using AI and alternative data.

### [[Idea 1 - Alt-Data Credit Scoring for Farmers|Idea 1: Alternative-Data Agri-Credit Scoring]]
- **Target Partner**: Community/Cooperative Banks (e.g., [[Partners/CARD MRI (Philippines)|CARD MRI]])
- **The Concept**: Digitalize paper logs using mobile OCR/LLMs, then enrich the data with weather history and satellite crop health indices to create a multi-modal creditworthiness score for smallholder farmers.
- **Finverse Mappings**: Data Access (Collection Barriers), Data Quality (Missing Contextual Data), Insight Generation.
- **Status**: Draft - Needs Validation.

### [[Idea 2 - Voice-Led Ledger for Micro-Merchants|Idea 2: Voice-Led Micro-Ledger]]
- **Target Partner**: Mission-driven Fintechs / Microfinance platforms (e.g., [[Partners/Amartha (Indonesia)|Amartha]])
- **The Concept**: A WhatsApp/Viber voice bot that lets micro-merchants do their bookkeeping in local languages via simple voice messages. The backend AI structures this transaction data and returns financial health feedback.
- **Finverse Mappings**: Resource Constraints (Analysis Capacity), Data Quality (Inconsistent Data), Insight Generation (Measuring Financial Health).
- **Status**: Draft - Needs Validation.

### [[Idea 3 - Parametric Micro-Insurance Registry|Idea 3: Parametric Micro-Insurance Registry]]
- **Target Partner**: Disaster Management Agencies, NGOs (e.g., [[Partners/PRADAN (India)|PRADAN]]), Cooperative Insurance Networks.
- **The Concept**: Use satellite weather databases and parametric triggers to instantly disburse micro-insurance payouts via mobile wallets (like GCash) during droughts, floods, or typhoons.
- **Finverse Mappings**: Data Access (Sharing Barriers, Limited Availability), Resource Constraints (Granular Data).
- **Status**: Draft - Needs Validation.

### [[Idea 4 - Subjective Well-being Tracker|Idea 4: Subjective Well-being & Stress Tracker]]
- **Target Partner**: Microfinance Institutions (MFIs) using group-lending models.
- **The Concept**: Conduct quick verbal well-being surveys during weekly credit officer visits, using NLP sentiment analysis to track borrowers' latent financial stress. Use this data to trigger proactive payment holidays or savings plans.
- **Finverse Mappings**: Data Quality (Subjective Data), Insight Generation (Measuring Financial Health, Actioning Insights).
- **Status**: Draft - Needs Validation.

## 📊 Evaluation Matrix

To help choose our primary direction, here is a comparison based on feasibility, impact, and partnership potential:

| Metric | Idea 1: Agri-Credit | Idea 2: Voice Ledger | Idea 3: Parametric Insurance | Idea 4: Subjective Stress |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pillar of Financial Health** | Economic Resilience / Planning | Daily Cashflow Management | Economic Resilience (Shocks) | Security & Control |
| **Tech Complexity** | Medium (OCR + APIs + Underwriting) | High (ASR + LLM Parsing) | Medium (Geospatial API + Rules) | Medium (NLP Sentiment + Survey) |
| **Partner Feasibility** | High (Coops are desperate for this) | High (Fintechs love chat-bots) | Medium (Requires insurance licenses) | High (MFIs focus on social impact) |
| **UX Friction** | Low (Used by field agents) | Very Low (Used by merchants) | Very Low (Automatic trigger) | Low (Used by field agents) |
| **APAC Relevance** | Extremely High (Smallholders) | Extremely High (Informal Retail) | Extremely High (Climate disasters) | High (Over-indebtedness) |
