# 🧩 Finverse Category: Data Quality & Cohesion

Back to MOC: [[Hackathon MOC]]

This category addresses the issues of messy, disconnected, or incomplete data, and the lack of human-centric context in quantitative numbers.

---

## 🛑 Specific Challenges & Mappings

### 1. Fragmented & Disconnected Systems
- **Description**: Client data is scattered across different branches, government identification files, and micro-lending groups with zero interoperability.
- **Hackathon Relevance**: Integrating disparate datasets into a unified profile.
- **Related Ideas**:
  * [[Idea 1 - Alt-Data Credit Scoring for Farmers|Idea 1: Alt-Data Credit Scoring]] (merging cooperative records with satellite weather feeds)

<a id="Inconsistent-&-Unreliable-Data"></a>
### 2. Inconsistent & Unreliable Data
- **Description**: Manual entry mistakes, missing fields, or gaps in transaction histories make it hard to train reliable predictive credit models.
- **Hackathon Relevance**: Building validation rules and low-friction collection methods that prevent entry errors at the source.
- **Related Ideas**:
  * [[Idea 2 - Voice-Led Ledger for Micro-Merchants|Idea 2: Voice-Led Micro-Ledger]] (using AI to convert speech to standardized transaction formats)

<a id="Missing-Contextual-and-Subjective-Data"></a>
### 3. Missing Contextual and Subjective Data
- **Description**: Standard databases don't capture qualitative aspects, such as a borrower's mental stress, domestic safety, community trust level, or local agricultural conditions.
- **Hackathon Relevance**: Designing systems that gather and analyze qualitative, voice-based, or peer-led subjective data.
- **Related Ideas**:
  * [[Idea 4 - Subjective Well-being Tracker|Idea 4: Subjective Well-being Tracker]] (using sentiment analysis and verbal stress surveys)
  * [[Idea 1 - Alt-Data Credit Scoring for Farmers|Idea 1: Alt-Data Credit Scoring]] (integrating peer-group cooperative trust ratings)

### 4. Weak Data Infrastructure
- **Description**: Regional cooperative banks and rural MFIs run on legacy database servers or offline spreadsheets without real-time data sync.
- **Hackathon Relevance**: Formulating lightweight cloud middleware or edge-computing databases that work offline and sync when connected to 3G/4G networks.
