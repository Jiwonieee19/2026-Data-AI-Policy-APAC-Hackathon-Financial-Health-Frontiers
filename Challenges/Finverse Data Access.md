# 🧩 Finverse Category: Data Access, Collection & Sharing Barriers

Back to MOC: [[Hackathon MOC]]

This category addresses the friction, regulations, and mechanical hurdles involved in capturing financial and alternative data from underserved populations.

---

## 🛑 Specific Challenges & Mappings

### 1. Limited Data Availability & Accessibility
- **Description**: Many vulnerable populations lack formal credit footprints (no bank account, no card transactions).
- **Hackathon Relevance**: We must source alternative data (weather, utility, mobile top-ups) to fill this gap.
- **Related Ideas**:
  * [[Idea 1 - Alt-Data Credit Scoring for Farmers|Idea 1: Alt-Data Credit Scoring]] (using crop health and weather data)
  * [[Idea 3 - Parametric Micro-Insurance Registry|Idea 3: Parametric Micro-Insurance]] (using satellite rainfall grids)

### 2. Legal & Privacy Constraints
- **Description**: Data protection acts (like Indonesia's PDP Law, India's DPDPA, and Philippines' DPA) restrict FSPs from sharing customer records with external platforms.
- **Hackathon Relevance**: We must build privacy-preserving architectures (e.g., zero-knowledge proofs, hashing, locally computed client ratings) to comply with local laws.
- **Related Ideas**:
  * [[Idea 3 - Parametric Micro-Insurance Registry|Idea 3: Parametric Micro-Insurance]] (secure shared registration database)

### 3. Trust Deficit & Low Incentives for Data Sharing
- **Description**: Consumers fear how their data will be used (predatory sales or surveillance), and FSPs guard their client lists as proprietary assets.
- **Hackathon Relevance**: Creating transparent consent mechanisms and showing clear value back to the client (e.g., lower interest rates or immediate disaster payouts).
- **Related Ideas**:
  * [[Idea 3 - Parametric Micro-Insurance Registry|Idea 3: Parametric Micro-Insurance]]

<a id="Paper-based-collection"></a>
### 4. Paper-based Data Collection is Difficult, Expensive, and Inconsistent
- **Description**: A massive portion of rural commerce and community-saving group dynamics exists only in paper ledgers, notebooks, or physical receipt slips.
- **Hackathon Relevance**: Using mobile-based computer vision (OCR) and lightweight LLMs to digitize physical records on-site.
- **Related Ideas**:
  * [[Idea 1 - Alt-Data Credit Scoring for Farmers|Idea 1: Alt-Data Credit Scoring]] (digitizing farmer notebook logs)
