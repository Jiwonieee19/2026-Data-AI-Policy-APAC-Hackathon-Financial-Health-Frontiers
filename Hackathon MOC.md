# 🚀 Hackathon 2026: Financial Health in APAC

Welcome to the central workspace for the **Financial Health Hackathon (Asia-Pacific)**. This dashboard serves as a **Map of Content (MOC)** to connect our brainstorming, partner options, and Finverse data challenges.

> [!abstract] **Core Mission**
> Design data and AI-led solutions to improve the financial health of underserved and underbanked populations in the Asia-Pacific region, partnering with local Financial Service Providers (FSPs).
> 
> *Financial Health = Daily Management + Economic Resilience + Long-term Planning + Financial Security.*

---

## 📅 Roadmap & Milestones
- [ ] **Phase 1: Registration & Concept Submission** (Deadline: **July 12, 2026**)
  - Develop core ideas and select the top 1-2 directions.
- [ ] **Phase 2: Partner Selection & Solution Plan** (Deadline: **August 28, 2026**)
  - Identify a partner organization (signed Letter of Intent required).
  - Conduct a Needs Assessment Interview.
  - Submit detailed solution plan.
- [ ] **Phase 3: Finalist Prototyping** (Sept 26 - Nov 3)
  - Travel to Manila for the in-person hackathon (Nov 4-6) to build and pitch the prototype.
