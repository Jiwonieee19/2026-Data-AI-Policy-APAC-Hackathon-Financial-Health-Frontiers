Overview of Hackathon:

- this is much more specific than just simply building "AI finance app"

- Its not "help users manage money", its actually "help financial institutions serve people better"

- our customer is not the USER of the app/solution, its the FSP (bank, lending company, digital bank, you named it)

- the org wants a practical solution that helps Financial Service Provider, to improve financial health of UNDERSERVE communities, meaning nga gusto nila mag expand ang FSPs to urban communities.

- they r trying to make people feel safe when hearing FSP, not matikally mo ingon nga Pangawrtahan rta ana (eg.  atleast 50% ata studs sa um alaws FSP)

Problem they ask to solve:

- **Barriers to data access and sharing;** most sa fsp fails to obtain/exchange data needed to understand the customer (customer have fragmented financial histories, missing financial records)

- **Poor data quality;** incomplete data, inconsistent records, outdated information

- **Limited analytical resources;** doesnt even need an explanation here

- **Turning insights into decisions;** even though naa silay informations sa customers, they dont know how to utilize those
